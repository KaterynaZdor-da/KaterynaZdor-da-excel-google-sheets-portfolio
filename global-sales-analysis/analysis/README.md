# Sales Efficiency Analysis Report (2010–2017)

## 📋 Project Overview
This study analyzes an 8-year dataset covering sales performance across **46 countries**, totaling **6,576,524 units sold**.

* **Total Net Profit:** $63,282.06
* **Key Trend:** Following a period of aggressive expansion and growth (2010–2012), the company peaked in 2012. After a brief correction in 2014, a **systemic recession** began, impacting all geographic regions simultaneously.

---

## 📊 ABC Analysis

### Group A (High Profitability)
* **Categories:** *Office Supplies, Household, Meat, Snacks, and Vegetables.*
* **Impact:** These 5 categories generate **80% of total profit**.
* **Highlight:** *Snacks* and *Vegetables* stand out by combining high profitability with high transaction frequency.

### Group B (High Volume / Traffic Builders)
* **Categories:** *Cosmetics, Cereal, and Fruits.*
* **Impact:** These items have the highest purchase frequency, driving customer traffic, but operate on low profit margins.

### Group C (Underperformers)
* **Category:** *Personal Care.*
* **Impact:** This category shows weak efficiency across all metrics, including low demand and minimal profit contribution.

---

## 🌍 Geography & Sales Channels

* **Market Leaders:** *Southern Europe* is the flagship region ($163.3M profit). *Ukraine* ranks in the **Top 3 most profitable markets** ($15.6M), demonstrating the high potential of large-scale markets.
* **Sales Mix:** A near-perfect balance between **Online (49%)** and **Offline (51%)** channels indicates a highly resilient business model adapted to modern retail trends.

---

## ⚙️ Operational Efficiency & Logistics

* **Logistics Performance:** The average shipping time is **25 days**. Analysis shows **no direct correlation** between delivery speed and profit ($R^2 = 0.007$), suggesting high customer loyalty to the product range regardless of wait times.
* **Daily Activity Patterns:**
    * **Friday:** Critical for *Cosmetics* ($24.6M spike).
    * **Monday:** Peak for *Household* goods ($17.8M).
    * **Weekends:** Highest activity for family-oriented categories (*Baby Food, Cereal*).

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

1.  **Profit Optimization:** Implement cross-selling strategies using *Cereal* and *Fruits* as "lead magnets" to sell high-margin items (*Household, Office Supplies*) through bundles and promotions.
2.  **Recession Management:** Conduct a deep-dive cost audit for *Cereal* and *Baby Food* categories, which saw the sharpest profit declines in 2017.
3.  **Growth Investment:** The *Snacks* category demonstrated anomalous growth (**+52%**) during a general market downturn. It is recommended to reallocate marketing budgets to further boost this direction.
4.  **Logistics Agility:** Align warehouse staffing and logistics schedules with peak demand days (increasing capacity for Thu-Fri and Sun-Mon shifts).
